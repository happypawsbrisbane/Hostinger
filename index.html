About Us

We are a small, trusted team of experienced pet sitters providing professional pet care services in Brisbane. We have a limited number of clients at any given time to ensure that all pets receive the attention and care of the same familiar faces.

Whether your pet has anxiety, is senior, or simply likes the routine of home, our pet sitting staff is dependable and will accommodate your pet's needs and your schedule. All of our pet sitters are carefully selected and trained to our high standards.
