Common Questions

My dog is excitable/reactive. Can they still attend?

We specialize in anxious and high-energy dogs. During the Meet & Greet, we will assess if a wedding environment is safe for them. If the reception is too loud, we may recommend a "Photos Only" package in a quiet area of the venue.

Do you groom them?

We are not professional groomers, but we do "styling." This means brushing, wiping eyes/paws, spritzing with dog-safe cologne, and dressing them in their attire.

How far do you travel?

We service all Brisbane venues. Venues in the Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, or Hinterland attract a travel surcharge of $1.50/km.
